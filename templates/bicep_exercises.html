Bicep Exercises

Double Biceps

The double biceps curl is a resistance training exercise primarily targeting the biceps muscles. It involves holding a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing upwards, and curling the weights towards the shoulders while keeping the elbows stationary. This movement effectively isolates and strengthens the biceps, contributing to overall arm development and strength.

Single Arm Biceps

The single arm biceps curl is a strength training exercise that targets the biceps muscle. It involves holding a dumbbell in one hand, palm facing upwards, and curling the weight towards the shoulder while keeping the elbow stationary. This exercise helps improve biceps strength and definition, promoting balanced muscle development in the arms.

BarBell Bicep Curl

The barbell biceps curl is a fundamental resistance training exercise for developing the biceps muscles. It involves holding a barbell with an underhand grip, palms facing upwards, and curling the bar towards the shoulders while keeping the elbows stationary. This exercise effectively isolates and strengthens the biceps, contributing to improved arm strength and muscle definition.
